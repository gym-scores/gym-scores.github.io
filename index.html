an elite gymnastics score repository

In 2022, kmkeirns and I started putting together spreadsheets of elite women's artistic gymnastics scores, something that's searchable and sortable and compiled all in one place. We've continued in subsequent seasons, adding men's artistic and rhythmic along the way. But Google Sheets gets unwieldy long before you reach the end of an elite season. Katherine's current senior document has over 50,000 scores recorded prior to 2025 Artistic World Championships.

What you're looking at now is an effort to present the data in those spreadsheets in a more viewer-friendly format. Complete breakdowns are presented whenever they were available. Each page of results can be sorted by any column. The search bar will filter any column. (Worth noting that search is fairly rudimentary. A search for "ITA" will also return "Vitaliy Guimaraes" because the name contains that string.)

In the menu bar above, you can find links to pages for each apparatus, including single- and two-vault scores, as well as all-around and all-around difficulty. In artistic, two versions exist for each, one with results from every meet and one showing only FIG-sanctioned meets. For rhythmic, only FIG-sanctioned meet results are collected.

The site is a work in progress. Right now only senior MAG and WAG scores are available. The next update will be junior MAG/WAG, followed by rhythmic.
